🌸 Du’a Quote Gallery

Beautiful reminders for the heart

“Rabbi zidni ilma.”
— My Lord, increase me in knowledge. (Qur’an 20:114)
“La hawla wa la quwwata illa billah.”
— There is no power or strength except with Allah.
“Wa tawakkaltu ‘alallah.”
— I place my trust in Allah. (Qur’an 11:56)
“Rabbighfir li, waliwalidayya.”
— My Lord, forgive me and my parents. (Surah Ibrahim 14:41)
“Hasbunallahu wa ni’mal wakeel.”
— Allah is sufficient for us, and He is the best disposer of affairs. (Qur’an 3:173)
“Inna ma’al usri yusra.”
— Verily, with hardship comes ease. (Qur’an 94:6)
“Allahumma inni as’aluka al-‘afiyah.”
— O Allah, I ask You for wellness.
“Astaghfirullah wa atubu ilayh.”
— I seek forgiveness from Allah and repent to Him.
“Rabbi yassir wa la tu’assir.”
— O Lord, make it easy and do not make it difficult.
